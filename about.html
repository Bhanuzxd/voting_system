About Our System

What is This Platform?

The Online Voting and Feedback System for Universities is a digital platform designed to make internal university elections, student polls, and academic feedback collection easier and more transparent. It eliminates the need for paper-based voting or manual feedback forms.

Purpose

Our goal is to ensure that every voice—students, faculty, and staff—is heard securely and anonymously. Whether it's voting for student council representatives or submitting course feedback, the system guarantees fairness and accuracy.

Key Features

  • Secure Online Voting
  • Anonymous Feedback Submission
  • User Role-Based Access (Admin, Faculty, Students)
  • Real-Time Results & Analytics
  • Mobile & Desktop Responsive Interface

Who Can Use This System?

This system is built for universities and colleges. It can be accessed by:

  • Students
  • Faculty Members
  • Administrative Staff
